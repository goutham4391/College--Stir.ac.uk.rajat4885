Download a prospectus

Download a prospectus for an introduction to studying at the University of Stirling.

Our prospectuses give you an overview of life and study at Stirling. Discover the wide variety of courses we have on offer and find out about our campus facilities, as well as the support you'll receive while studying. A degree from the University of Stirling can help you achieve your potential and pursue your career.

Our prospectuses were produced as printed publications. They have not been made digitally accessible; however, all content within is available in an accessible format on our course pages.
