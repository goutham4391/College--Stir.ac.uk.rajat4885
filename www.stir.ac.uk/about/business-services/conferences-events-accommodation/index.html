Conferences, events and accommodation

We host everything from meetings and training sessions to prestigious regional, national and international events. Whether you're planning a business conference or organising a gala dinner, let us help make your event a success.

University of Stirling Venues

All our conference and event facilities are managed by University of Stirling Venues, our dedicated conference team. They will support you throughout the preparation and on the day to ensure your event is a success. Go to the University of Stirling Venues website for all the information you need to plan your event.

University of Stirling Venues

Why choose the University of Stirling for your conference or event?

We have some of the most extensive meeting, event and accommodation facilities on site anywhere in Scotland, including:

  • ten major lecture theatres, including the 460-seat Macrobert theatre auditorium
  • seminar/break out rooms with high specification presentation facilities
  • a dedicated exhibition area
  • 26 conference and meeting rooms in the Stirling Court Hotel
  • the 100-bedroom Stirling Court Hotel with a mix of double, twin and accessible rooms
  • single rooms, flats, chalets and apartments are available during the summer months
  • a range of private dining options for large corporate events
  • exclusive use of the Iris Murdoch conference suite

Accommodation

Here at the University of Stirling we have a wide range of accommodation available all year round whether you're looking for conference accommodation or somewhere to enjoy a short break.

Stirling Court Hotel

The hotel is now open. It is crucial to us that we deliver a safe and comfortable space that you have grown to love at Stirling Court Hotel. We have been working tirelessly to ensure that this is delivered to you every time you come to visit. This includes gaining our Good to Go Accreditation from Visit Scotland.

Our Staying Safely charter has been created to reassure all of our guests of a safe and warm welcome from us.

Whether you’re looking for a little rest and relaxation, a dinner out with friends or a picture perfect wedding venue, Stirling Court Hotel is a haven of Scottish hospitality.

Go to Stirling Court Hotel website

Holiday accommodation

We have over 1500 beds available from early June until early September. This includes a range of brand new en-suite single and double rooms and larger double studios. We also offer accommodation in our chalets and apartments that are perfect for group or family bookings.

The majority of our accommodation is located within the University’s 330 acres of beautiful parkland campus, with additional accommodation located in the City of Stirling.

With such a wide range of accommodation available there's something to suit everyone's holiday tastes.

Use of University Grounds

If you would like to request the use of grounds within the University and/or use University facilities, please complete the form below. 

Examples of when a Use of Grounds Request should be made include

  • exclusive use of car parks
  • charity walks
  • use of Airthrey Loch
  • filming and photography

A use of grounds request should be made as early as possible to avoid disappointment. We'll contact you within seven working days of receipt of the completed form to update you on the progress of your request.

We may ask you to provide additional information and/or a risk assessment to support your application. A fee may be charged depending on the exact requirements of the request.

Please fill in our form if you want to hold an event on the University campus and/or use University facilities.
