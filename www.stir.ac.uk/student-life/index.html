Student life

Studying at the University of Stirling is an unforgettable experience, but don’t just take our word for it. We're 1st in the UK and 5th in the world for our campus environment (UK International Student Barometer (ISB) Benchmark and Global ISB Benchmark 2018)

We’re a close-knit community with a global outlook, and no matter where you’re from or hope to end up, we think you’ll like it here.

1st in the UK and 5th in the World for campus environment

UK International Student Barometer (ISB) Benchmark and Global ISB Benchmark 2018
