Alumni and supporters

Since the University of Stirling opened its doors in 1967, our alumni family has grown to more than 100,000 people in 170 countries. The Advancement Team are dedicated to friend- and fund-raising through building relationships with alumni, friends, retired staff and benefactors to inspire support for a world-class research institute that prepares the leaders of tomorrow.

Our fundraising activities support students, widening access, employability, internationalisation, research, capital projects and the University Art Collection – all of which continue to improve the Stirling student experience. Explore the links below to update your details, get in touch with old friends, discover the perks of being a Stirling graduate, or make a contribution that will benefit the students following in your footsteps.
