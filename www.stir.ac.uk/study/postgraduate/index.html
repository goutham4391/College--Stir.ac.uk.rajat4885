Postgraduate study

Complete one of our Masters courses in just one year to achieve your career objectives and realise your life ambitions. We have 90 postgraduate degrees for you to choose from.

View all postgraduate courses

Types of taught courses

At Stirling, we’re all about choice. We offer a range of qualifications including MSc, MA, MRes and MBA. Most of our Masters courses take one year to complete.

Research degrees

We offer Master of Philosophy (MPhil), Doctor of Philosophy (PhD) and Professional Doctorates. See which research degree is right for you and your career ambitions.
