Scotland's University for Sporting Excellence

Our mission is to deliver the best for Scottish sport through the powerful and inspirational combination of sport and education.

Stirling has been Scotland’s University for Sporting Excellence since 2008, and was awarded the UK’s Sports University of the Year by The Times and Sunday Times Good University Guide 2020.
