Visit us

Our stunning 330-acre campus is located in the centre of Scotland and attracts students and their families to the University from all over the world.

But don't just take our word for it. We encourage prospective students, their families and supporters to visit our beautiful campus, learn more about the University and see our first-rate study, sport and social facilities for themselves.

There's lots of different ways you can come and see if Stirling is the place for you:

  • Campus tours
  • Open days
  • Virtual tour
  • Meet our recruitment team
  • Offer holder day

Campus tours

We are offering the opportunity for prospective students, applicants and their families to visit the campus. Visits take place every Monday, Wednesday and Friday, subject to availability. The University follows guidance from the Scottish Government on COVID-19 mitigations and safety measures. Campus tour dates therefore may be subject to change, however we will endeavour to give as much notice as possible should a tour have to be cancelled.

Our socially distanced campus tours, led by trained Student Ambassadors, will last up to 90 minutes. The tours allow you to see our fantastic facilities and beautiful campus for yourself, giving you a sense of what it will be like to live and study at Stirling.

Book your place

Booking is compulsory. Spaces on tours are limited, so please book a minimum of five working days prior to your preferred visit date.

Meet us

Our student recruitment team attend many different events and exhibitions across the UK. Come along and meet the team to ask any questions you have about the University of Stirling or higher education. Meet us at UCAS exhibitions throughout the year.

Are you a teacher, lecturer or career adviser?

Our recruitment team are here to help you with any aspects of Higher Education or the University of Stirling. We visit schools, colleges and universities, and attend events throughout the UK. Get in touch with the team to find out if we’re attending one near you, or if you have an event you would like us to attend.

Offer Holder Day

All undergraduate offer holders will be invited to our Offer Holder Day which gives you a taste of life as a Stirling student. You can find out more about your course and chat to staff and students. You'll also be able to explore our beautiful campus.

In 2023 our on-campus day will be on 15 April 2023, 8.30am - 3pm and our virtual offer holder day is on 29 April 2023, 2pm – 4pm.
