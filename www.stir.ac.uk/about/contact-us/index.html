Contact us

See contact details for a range of services at the University of Stirling, including media enquiries, studying, conference bookings and more.

General enquiries

You can get in touch with the University of Stirling by calling +44 (0)1786 473171.

British Sign Language (BSL) users can contact the University of Stirling via contactSCOTLAND-BSL, the online British Sign Language video relay interpreting service. Find out more on the contactSCOTLAND website.

Studying at the University

If you have any questions about studying with us at the University of Stirling please get in touch using our 'Ask a question' form. You can also chat to our student ambassadors about any aspects of student life such as accommodation, nightlife, the best places to eat and drink, and what’s on in Stirling.
