International Summer School

Our dynamic International Summer School gives you a rewarding, once-in-a-lifetime experience, through our well-rounded education, social and cultural programme. Experience student life at the University, while gaining credits towards your course at your home institution.

We know you'll leave with more confidence, fantastic friends and an experience you’ll never forget.

Learn how to apply

What can I study?

Our course modules incorporate a distinctive Scottish flavour, and also allow for critical thinking of international themes.

What's included?

Our programme includes academic tuition, excursions, on-campus accommodation. fun day trips and social activities.

Fees

Our fees depend on how long you want to study with us, and how many credits you want to take.

Why choose Stirling International Summer School?

Our programmes are about so much more than studying. Whether you’re looking to spend your time here studying interesting modules or undertaking work experience, we’re sure you’ll have an unforgettable summer.
