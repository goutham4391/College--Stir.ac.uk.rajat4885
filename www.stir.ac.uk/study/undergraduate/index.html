Undergraduate study

View all undergraduate courses

At the University of Stirling, we're committed to helping you to make a positive difference in the world.

Our flexible degree courses will help you to be the difference in your chosen career, by providing you with the knowledge, expertise and lifelong employability skills to ensure you stand out from the crowd. You'll be taught by our world-leading academics, whose internationally renowned research is tackling some of the biggest global challenges.

We offer an unrivalled student experience to help you make the most of your time with us. Our beautiful 330-acre campus offers a wide range of accommodation, and everything you need is just a few minutes’ walk away. As Scotland’s University for Sporting Excellence, we provide the highest-quality sports facilities for our students, high-performance and Olympic athletes, staff and the wider community.

Why Stirling for undergraduate study?

The University of Stirling is a great place for you to study. You won’t just earn a qualification – you’ll discover a welcoming and thriving undergraduate student community. See what Stirling is all about.
