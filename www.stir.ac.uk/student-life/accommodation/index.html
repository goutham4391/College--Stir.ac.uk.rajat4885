Our range of secure and affordable accommodation includes over 2,000 rooms right on our beautiful campus, as well as easy-to-reach off-campus residences. Why is University-managed accommodation the best choice?

Location, location

Be close to all your classes, supermarket, health centre, restaurants, cinema, library, Students' Union and Sports Centre.

No extra bills

Heating, electricity, contents insurance, WiFi, bedding and housekeeping are all included in your rent.

24/7 security and support

Our accommodation has trained staff on hand 24/7 to offer you their full support and assistance.

Availability

We have over 2,000 affordable rooms for you to choose from.

If you choose private accommodation, please note there is limited private accommodation available in Scotland for multiple occupancy. There are also laws in Scotland relating to the number of students that are legally allowed to share a property. You should try to find accommodation that is local to the University to ensure you don't incur expensive travel costs to and from campus.
