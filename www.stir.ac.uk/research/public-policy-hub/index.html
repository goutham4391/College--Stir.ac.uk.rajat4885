Public policy hub

The University of Stirling is a research-led institution, delivering research that has a positive impact on communities worldwide: informing policy, influencing decisions, and changing lives. Our researchers work closely with government, parliamentarians and civil society, placing evidence and academic rigour at the heart of policymaking.

The Stirling public policy hub brings together the latest insights, evidence and commentary from our researchers, in a one-stop-shop for policymakers. By connecting politicians, decision makers, and practitioners with our world-leading research, we can help deliver evidence-based policy that meets the grand challenges facing society, from climate change and food security, to intergenerational wellbeing.
