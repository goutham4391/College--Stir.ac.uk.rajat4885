Students' Union

Helping to ensure students’ voices are always heard here at the University of Stirling, our Students’ Union exists to champion the causes that matter most to you.  

The Union is a democratically elected body led by students, for students, and works to enhance your experience and opportunities at uni and beyond. From offering academic representation and support, to overseeing clubs and award-winning facilities, the Students’ Union plays a key role in creating a positive, inclusive environment for all.

Making students' lives better

Take a closer look at the Union’s venues and services, see an A-Z of clubs and find out how you can get involved at the Stirling Students' Union website.
