Find your Clearing place at the University of Stirling, Scotland

We’re an international research university based in the heart of Scotland, UK. Stirling is home to some of Scotland’s most famous historical sites and tourist attractions. 

Our students gain more than just a degree. We give you the support and advice you need to plan your career and develop your employability. And our degree flexibility puts you in control of your education. You can explore new subjects, combine the ones that interest you or even change your mind about what you’d like to graduate in.
