University events

University of Stirling events are back with a calendar of in-person and virtual events including open days, webinars and exhibitions. We also have our "Be Connected" programme with live, in-person and virtual events, activities and resources for our Stirling community of students and staff.

Many events at the University are open to the public and free of charge, but we do ask you to register for some events in advance. We look forward welcoming you to our events.

Be Connected

Discover a programme of events, activities and resources for our Stirling community of students and staff. Activities range from live workouts hosted by our Sports Centre staff and friendly drop in coffee sessions, to craft workshops and meditation sessions.

Webinars

Thinking about studying at the University of Stirling? Join one of our webinars to find out more about our exciting courses. Our Course Directors will provide you with content which includes:

  • An overview of the course
  • Module content
  • Course-relevant career opportunities
  • Student life on campus
  • Living in Stirling
  • Funding and scholarships
  • Q&A session with the course coordinator

Special requirements

Parking charges apply to all campus car parks and affect all who park on campus. Find out more about visitor parking.
